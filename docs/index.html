Get started in 30 seconds

  1. Download the app for your computer from the link above
  2. Enter your inverter's IP address in Settings (or click Scan Network to find it automatically)
  3. Click Connect — live data appears immediately

Everything you need

📊

Real-time Dashboard

Solar generation, battery charge, grid import/export, and home consumption — updating live every few seconds.

⚡

Energy Flow Diagram

Animated visual showing where your power is flowing right now — solar, battery, home, grid.

🔋

Battery Detail

Cell voltages, temperatures, and health for every battery module. Cold battery warnings included.

📅

Charge Schedules

Up to 10 charge and 10 discharge time slots. Switch between Eco, Timed Discharge, and Pause modes.

🛡️

Manual Overrides

Force Charge and Force Discharge buttons for instant control. Click to start, click again to stop.

📄

PDF Consumption Reports

Generate formatted A4 PDF reports with charts, summary tables, and cost breakdowns — one click from the History page.

📈

History & Costs

Charts from 15 minutes to 7 days. Enter your tariffs to see real-time cost estimates. Export to CSV or PDF.

🐙

Octopus Cosy (beta)

Enter your three cheap-rate windows and the app automatically charges your battery during each one.

💷

Octopus Agile (beta)

Set price thresholds and the app charges when prices are low, discharges when high, with a live 24-hour forecast.

❄️

Auto Winter Mode

Protects your battery from cold by automatically charging it from the grid when the temperature drops.

☀️

Solar Monitoring

Voltage, current, and power for each solar string. Supports dual-string systems.

📐

Meters Page

External meter readings with per-phase data and CT clamp status card.

📱

Access From Anywhere

Works as a headless server on a Raspberry Pi. Access from your phone via Tailscale — no cloud needed.

🔔

Alert Notifications

Get alerted when battery temperature, SOC, or solar exceeds thresholds — via Telegram, ntfy, or Pushover push notifications. Send /status, /today, or /report in your Telegram chat for a live snapshot, today's cost summary, or yesterday's full consumption report.
